About Us

Hedge Pensions Trust (HPT) is a company established to offer short term and long term financial and investment planning services for the staff of the Civil Service and the Local Government Service of Ghana (CLOGSAG).
HPT’s competence hinges on:Confidentiality, which is one of the most important aspect of the total services the company offers. We guarantee the privacy of three essential key components.

Mission

To achieve operational excellence, superior financial performance, first class customer satisfaction and secured pension income.

Vision

Hedge Pensions Trust exist to provide adequate and dignified retirement package to each member through safe, prudent and strategic investment policies
